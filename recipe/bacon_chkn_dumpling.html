Recipe for Bacon, Chicken and Dumpling Soup

Description

Perfect for those less than warm days, our bacon, chicken and dumpling soup will give you that cozy feeling. The rich creaminess envelope the potatoes and
chicken, while the bacon provides unique saltiness. And if that wasn't already enough, the onion and corn provide that wonderful, subtle sweetness that just
makes you want to take another bite. The recipe will be so good, I'm sure this won't your last visit here!

Ingredients

  • 3 slices bacon
  • 3 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - diced
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained and rinsed
  • 3 cups half-and-half
  • 1 Â½ cups biscuit mix
  • 1 cup milk

Steps for cooking

  1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside; reserve bacon drippings in skillet.

  2. Add potatoes, onion and chicken to bacon drippings and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Pour in chicken broth; season with poultry seasoning,
    and pepper. Stir in corn, and simmer all together for 15 minutes.

  3. Pour in half-and-half and bring to a boil; add crumbled bacon. In a medium bowl, combine biscuit mix with milk and mix well (dough should be thick).
    Drop tablespoon sizes of dough into boiling mixture; reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes uncovered, then another 10 minutes covered.
    (Note: Do not stir while simmering, or dumplings will break apart). Serve hot.
